## Repository-verified data practices

- Village progress, settings, processed StoreKit transaction identifiers, and a
  bounded support-report history are stored locally on the device.
- Apple StoreKit processes consumable Gem purchases. Monkey Tribe does not store
  card details or Apple Account credentials.
- Support emails are created in the player's mail app and can include the selected
  category, app version/build, OS version, device model, and an optional player note.
- The current app contains no advertising, analytics, cross-app tracking, or
  third-party crash-reporting SDK.
- The public legal site is hosted by Vercel.

The public policy contains the complete disclosures for retention, deletion
requests, children's privacy, international users, third parties, and contact.
