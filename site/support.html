Reporting a problem

To help us fix your issue fast, please include the following in your message:

  • Device — e.g. iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S24
  • Operating system — e.g. iOS 19.2, Android 15
  • Game version — shown on the App Store / Google Play page for Monkey Tribe
  • What happened — a short description of the problem and, if possible, the steps that trigger it. Screenshots help a lot!

Tip: the in-game Help & Support screen (Settings → Help & Support) can prepare this email for you and automatically attaches a small game-state summary, which you can review before sending.

Common questions

My progress disappeared

Game progress is stored on your device. Deleting the app also deletes your village — reinstalling starts a fresh game. If your progress vanished without deleting the app, email us with your device details.

No sound

Check the in-game sound toggle (Settings → Sound) and make sure your device isn't in silent mode.
