Reporting a bug

The fastest route is the in-game Help & Support screen (Settings → Help & Support): it opens a pre-filled email that you can review and edit before sending. If you'd rather write to us directly, please describe what happened and, if possible, the steps that trigger it. Screenshots help a lot.

Device information

When writing to us directly, include:

  • Device — e.g. iPhone 15, Samsung Galaxy S24
  • Operating system — e.g. iOS 19.2, Android 15
  • Game version — shown on the store page for Monkey Tribe

Emails started from the in-game Help & Support screen automatically include your app version and build number, platform, device model and the issue category you selected — nothing more — and you can review the full message before it is sent.

Purchases and billing

All purchases are billed by the Apple App Store through your Apple account; we never handle your payment details. Refund requests are also handled by Apple — you can submit one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Gem packs are consumable purchases: the Gems are added to your balance as soon as the purchase completes. Consumables are not covered by a standard "Restore Purchases" option, and Gem balances currently cannot be transferred to another device or a new save.

Payment completed but no Gems

Restart the game first. If your balance is still missing the Gems, email us with the purchase date, the product name (the Gem pack you bought), your game version and your device model, and we'll look into it.

Purchase pending

If the App Store shows your purchase as pending, the Gems are delivered once Apple finishes processing the payment. If it stays pending for more than a day, check your payment method in your Apple account, or contact us with the details above.

When helping with a purchase we only ever ask for the purchase date, product name, game version and device model — never your card number or any other payment credentials.

Frequently asked questions

My progress disappeared

Progress is stored on your device, so deleting the app also deletes your village and reinstalling starts a fresh game. If your progress vanished without deleting the app, email us with your device details.

Do I need an account?

No. Monkey Tribe doesn't use accounts or sign-ins — your village is saved directly on your device.

There's no sound

Check the in-game sound toggle (Settings → Sound) and make sure your device isn't in silent mode.
